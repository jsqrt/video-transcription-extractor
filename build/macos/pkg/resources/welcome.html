Welcome to the Describely installer

Describely transcribes and summarizes video and audio files entirely on your Mac. No internet, no cloud, no telemetry.

This installer will:

  • Copy Describely.app into /Applications.
  • Register two Finder right-click items: Create Transcription and Create Summary.
  • Launch Describely once the install completes.

No personal data is collected. All processing stays on this computer.
